How We Met.

Kimberly and Anthony have a mutual friend’s birthday party to thank for their love story. In April 2015, the two of them sat across from one another at a hibachi restaurant where the celebration took place. “Anthony kept blowing me kisses while winking at me the entire night to get my attention,” Kimberly recalls. “I usually hate encounters like that, but there was something different that kept me interested even though I was embarrassed at the time.” A few months later, Kimberly went out with friends and saw Anthony on the dance floor. That rekindling prompted endless texts from Anthony in the ensuing months. “He had asked to take me out several times before I finally said ‘yes,’” Kimberly notes. “Anthony and I have been inseparable ever since.”

Our Story..

After Maren and Anthony met in high school in August 2012, they kept each other in the friend zone for the remaining four years—and the following four years of college. Despite their non-romantic relationship status, the two of them kept in close contact and visited each other a few times at their respective schools. It wasn’t until after they graduated that Maren and Anthony decided to turn their friendship into a romance, which started out as long-distance until Maren moved to Miami, where Anthony was living.

How We Met.

When Courtney was a sophomore in high school who had just been dumped by her boyfriend, she took to an on-campus bench to process the news. Andrew, a senior at the time, saw Courtney crying and asked if she was OK. “From there, our friendship started,” Courtney says. Ever since then, Andrew had a crush on Courtney, but she dated other people. “I came back for summer after my freshman year of college, and he finally asked me on a date,” Courtney reveals. “We’ve been together ever since.”
